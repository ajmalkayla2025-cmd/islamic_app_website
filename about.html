Our Mission

In an age of digital distraction, we set out to build an Islamic application that respects your focus and your privacy. Islamic App is not just a tool; it's a companion designed to help you maintain your daily spiritual routine without the noise of intrusive ads or unnecessary data tracking.

We believe that religious technology should be pure, accurate, and accessible to everyone, regardless of where they are in the world.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% Ad-Free Experience: We prioritize your peace of mind over profit.
  • Privacy-Centric: We don't sell your data. We don't track your history.
  • Global Accuracy: Powered by the Aladhan API and precise astronomical calculations.

Core Principles

Everything we build is guided by these three foundational values.

Privacy First

As outlined in our Privacy Policy, your location data is used fleetingly for calculations and never stored or shared for marketing.

Technical Precision

We use industry-standard astronomical formulas to ensure that your prayer times, Qibla direction, and calendar events are precise.

Community Focused

We listen to our users. From adding new calculation methods to refining the UI, this app evolves based on the needs of the Ummah.
