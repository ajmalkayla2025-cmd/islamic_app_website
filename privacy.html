Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 07, 2026

Effective Date: January 07, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Islamic App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

  • Purpose: Prayer times, Qibla direction, and nearby mosque discovery.
  • Collection: While the app is in use (foreground).
  • Limited Updates: When enabled, location may be updated via a foreground service to keep prayer times accurate while traveling.
  • Sharing: Sent securely to the Aladhan API only for prayer calculations.
  • Storage: No continuous tracking and no location history is stored.

2.2 Device & Diagnostic Data

  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics for stability improvements
  • Advertising Support: We display ads to support our free services. Standard advertising identifiers may be used by third-party ad networks.

2.3 Prayer Logs & Preferences

  • Prayer status (Completed, Missed, Qada)
  • Calculation methods and notification preferences
  • Stored securely using anonymous Firebase Authentication

3. Android Permissions & Foreground Services

Permissions

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
  • WAKE_LOCK
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS

Foreground Services

  • mediaPlayback – uninterrupted Azan playback
  • location – prayer time updates while traveling
  • dataSync – prayer log synchronization

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – Authentication, Firestore, Crash Reporting
  • Aladhan API – Prayer time calculations
  • Google Maps – Mosque finder functionality

5. Data Security

All data is stored securely using Google Cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct or delete your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability (where applicable)
  • We do not sell or share personal data
  • No discrimination for exercising privacy rights

7. Children’s Privacy

Islamic App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a revised date.
